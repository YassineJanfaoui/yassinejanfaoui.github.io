About us:

This is a website project. It is a website about animals, animal health and adoption centers in Tunisia. This website is meant to raise awareness about the dangers that animals face in the streets of our beloved country and even in our households. I made it using the many skills I learnt.

Our mission:

Tunisia nowadays faces a rampant phenomenon of animal abuse in the streets or even in households unfortunately. So, this website hhopefully sheds light on this phenomenon while also giving names of organizations that fight this problem and adoption centers that provide stray animals with the healthcare and families they deserve.
