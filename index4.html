Animal abuse

The statistical chart above showcases the rising of animal abuse over the years in the United States and although the number has been decreasing since 2007 the fact that there are still animals in the streets being tortured and treated unfairly is truly a heartbreaking reality.Even though this graph shows animal abuse numbers in the US, Tunisia is no different and it could be in fact worse than in the US. Animal abuse can be in the form of:
-Beating the animal.
-Not feeding and not giving it water.
-neglecting its freedom and necessities.
So,this website was created to at least let people know of this phenomenon and help defend animal rights and well-being by showing you centers that fight against the unjust treatment of animals such as Adoption centers and non governmental organizations.
